# Movie Skip

Movie skip, also known as Money skip, is an alternative to Honey skip that is often faster for beginners.

### HUD Line-Up
#### Wii/GCN Visual Cues
- Line up the X button from fludd with the corner of the square on the right portion of the raised roof.
- OR line up the corner of the M Graffiti wall with the left side of the coin counter
- Climb up and jumpdive with neutral analog stick
- Savewarp with the blue coin textbox

#### 3DAS Visual Cues
- Line up the transition line between the dark part of the wall and the light part of the wall between the stick-out portion of the Q in Acqua
- OR line up the corner of the M Graffiti wall to be slightly right of the top part of the left 0 in the coin counter
- Climb up and jumpdive with neutral analog stick
- Savewarp with the blue coin textbox
